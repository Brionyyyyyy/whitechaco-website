About Us

"Asian restaurant in Braddon"
White Chaco is a cozy, welcoming bar located in the heart of Braddon, Canberra.
Known for its unique fusion of Taiwanese and Japanese flavors, White Chaco offers a relaxed atmosphere with creative cocktails and delicious dishes that are both beautiful and satisfying.
